First Study to Detect the First Heartbeat in a Vertebrate

Megason and Cohen Labs collaborate to detect the first heartbeat in zebrafish by elegantly combining methods across disciplines.

Jia et al., Megason and Cohen, Nature (2023)
Science

Definitive Evidence for the Differential Adhesion Hypothesis

The Megason Lab uses innovative imaging techniques combined with micropipettes, aspirating air to hold onto single cells, to provide the first direct biophysical evidence for the differential adhesion hypothesis.

Tsai et al., Megason, Science (2020)
Science

Symmetry Breaking in Gastruloids is Driven by Differential Adhesion

The Toettcher Lab combines gastruloids with a synthetic signaling reporter, which itself combines Cre lineage tracing with signaling reporters, to show that symmetry breaking in gastruloids is driven by differential adhesion downstream of developmental signaling pathways.

McNamara et al., Toettcher, Nat. Cel. Bio. (2024)

Poetry

Wislawa Szymborska's Nobel Prize Speech

A beautiful speech from a beautiful poet about what it means to be a poet in today's world, and the importance of being able to say "I don't know"--for poets and scientists alike. "This is why I value that little phrase "I don't know" so highly. It's small, but it flies on mighty wings. It expands our lives to include the spaces within us as well as those outer expanses in which our tiny Earth hangs suspended."
